Peace Café is a student-led platform under the Centre for Peace and Justice (CPJ), BRAC University. Established in 2021 and officially launched in 2022, Peace Café empowers students through leadership development, peacebuilding initiatives, social justice advocacy, community engagement, and meaningful social action.

PEACE CAFÉ × CPJ

Part of a Larger Movement for Peace and Justice

Peace Café operates under the Centre for Peace and Justice (CPJ), BRAC University. CPJ is dedicated to advancing peace, justice, inclusion, and social cohesion through research, education, dialogue, advocacy, and community engagement. Through Peace Café, students gain opportunities to participate in meaningful initiatives that connect academic learning with real-world social impact.
