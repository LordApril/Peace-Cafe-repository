About Peace Café

Peace Café is a student-led platform under the Centre for Peace and Justice (CPJ), BRAC University. Established in 2021 and officially launched in 2022, it empowers students through leadership development, peacebuilding, community engagement, and social impact initiatives.

Peace Café × CPJ
